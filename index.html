Sports Day Website

MGS Sports Day Website is offline until 2019

Thank you for using the MGS Sports Day website 2018, the site which allows you to view results and competitors from the MGS Senior School Sports Day in real time. It has now been taken offline until Sports Day 2019, so that development work may take place.

Archived results are still avaliable on Google Sheets. Please click the relevant year to view results: (You will be re-directed to Google Sheets to do this.): 2017 2018

If you wish to view the development site, you can do so here: https://tti0.github.io/sportsday. Please note, however, that the development site is not stable and does not use live data.

For more site information, including a Privacy Policy, please click the button below.
