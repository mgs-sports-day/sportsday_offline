Thank you for using the MGS Sports Day website 2019, the site which allows you to view results and competitors from the MGS Senior School Sports Day in real time. It has now been taken offline until Sports Day 2020, and this year's results hidden, so that development work may take place.

This website is part of a family of co-curricular sites run by students and staff of the Manchester Grammar School. The main school website is at mgs.org.
