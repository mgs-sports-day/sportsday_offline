MGS Sports Day Website is offline until 2021

Thank you for using the MGS Sports Day website 2019, the site which allows you to view results and records from the MGS Senior School Sports Day in real time. It has now been taken offline until Sports Day 2021, so that development work may take place.

Archived results are still available on Google Sheets. Please click the relevant year to view results:

  • 2016 (No Sports Day)
  • 2017
  • 2018
  • 2019
  • 2020 (No Sports Day)
This website is part of a family of co-curricular sites run by students and staff of the Manchester Grammar School. The main school website is at mgs.org.
